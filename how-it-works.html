The Process

From text to tee time in minutes.

No app switching. No phone tag. No group chat chaos. Here's exactly how TeeSync works — start to finish.

Step by Step

Three steps to your next round.

TeeSync streamlines the whole process — from deciding to play to stepping on the first tee.

01

Send an Invitation

Open TeeSync, browse live tee time availability at local courses, choose the one that works, and send an invite to your group — all inside iMessage.

  • Browse courses and live availability
  • Choose your date, time, and player count
  • Send the TeeSync invite in one tap

02

Group Accepts or Declines

Everyone receives a TeeSync invitation through iMessage and taps to accept or decline. The host sees responses live — no back-and-forth, no confusion.

  • One tap to accept or decline
  • Live response updates for the host
  • Everyone stays automatically in the loop

03

Host Books for Everyone

Once the group is locked in, the host books the tee time for the whole group in seconds. Confirmations, payment reminders, and weather updates go out automatically.

  • Host confirms and books for the full group
  • Instant booking confirmation sent to all
  • Automatic reminders leading up to the round
  • Weather update the morning of your tee time

Built-In Tools

Everything your group needs.

TeeSync goes beyond booking — it handles coordination, follow-up, and day-of details so you can focus on your game.

Live Group Availability

See who's in at a glance. Responses update in real-time so the host always has a clear picture before pulling the trigger on a booking.

Course Search

Browse and compare local courses without leaving the app. View availability, pricing, and course details side-by-side before you commit.

Smart Reminders

Automated reminders, payment nudges, and a weather update the morning of your round — so everyone shows up on time and ready to play.
