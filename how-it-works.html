The Process

Easy as Sending a Text.

TeeSync makes tee time booking as simple as 1 message. Here's exactly how it works — from suggesting a round to stepping on the first tee.

Step by Step

Three steps to your next round.

No app switching. No phone tag. No group chat chaos. TeeSync lives inside iMessage so your booking flow stays inside your conversation.

01

Send an Invitation

Open TeeSync, pick your course and tee time, and send an invitation to your group — all without leaving iMessage.

  • Browse courses & available times
  • Choose your date and player count
  • Send the invite in one tap
  • TeeSync handles the rest
02

Accept or Decline

Everyone in the thread receives a TeeSync invitation and taps to accept or decline — right inside iMessage.

  • One tap to accept or decline
  • Live response updates
  • Everyone stays in the loop
  • No back-and-forth needed
03

Host Books for Everyone

Once the group confirms, the host books the tee time for the whole group in seconds — confirmations, reminders, and payment nudges go out automatically.

  • Host books for the whole group
  • Instant booking confirmation
  • Automatic reminders sent
  • Payment reminders & weather updates
Tee Time Alerts

Never miss a prime time again.

Tired of prime tee time spots getting snatched before you even wake up? TeeSync monitors courses 24/7 and alerts you the moment a qualifying slot opens — so you can book it before anyone else does.

Set Your Preferences
Choose your course, date range, time window, and player count. Set it once — TeeSync watches it for you around the clock.
We Monitor Openings
Continuous checks for newly released tee times and last-minute cancellations that match your exact filters.
Instant Alert
The second a qualifying slot opens, you're notified. Speed wins prime tee times — TeeSync makes sure you're always first.
Additional Features

More Than Just Easy Booking.

TeeSync offers a set of tools that make planning smoother from start to finish.

Shared Availability

See who's free at a glance. Availability is updated live so you can pinpoint the best tee time for your entire group before committing.

Built-In Course Search

Browse and select from local courses without opening another app. Compare availability, pricing, and course details side-by-side.

Painless Follow-Up

Automated reminders keep everyone informed leading up to the round — including weather updates the morning of your tee time.
