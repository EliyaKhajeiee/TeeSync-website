Why TeeSync

Book Smarter. Not Harder.

TeeSync removes the friction from planning, booking, and confirming your round — all inside iMessage.

Transparent Pricing

TeeSync never marks up tee times. All prices are set directly by the course — what you see is exactly what you pay. No hidden fees. No surprise charges.

Built-In Course Search

Browse courses, compare availability, and view pricing without leaving your conversation. Everything you need is built directly into the flow.

Automatic Group Coordination

No more "are we still on?" texts. TeeSync handles confirmations, updates, and reminders so everyone in your group stays aligned.
