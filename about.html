Our Story

Built by Golfers,
for Golfers.

TeeSync started from a shared frustration — planning a round with friends shouldn't feel like running a logistics operation.

What We're Building

Golf should be simple to plan.

TeeSync is a golf technology platform designed to simplify how golfers discover and book tee times. Instead of searching across multiple golf course websites and booking systems, TeeSync aggregates available tee times into a single platform where players can easily view availability, compare pricing, and secure bookings.

Our goal is to streamline the tee time discovery process, reduce friction for players, and make golf more accessible by making open tee times easier to find.

The core of what we've built is an iMessage-native booking system that lets people share and confirm tee times directly inside their conversations — making group bookings as seamless as sending a text.
