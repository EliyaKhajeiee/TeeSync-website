TeeSync is a golf app that makes it effortless to coordinate and book tee times with a group. Instead of juggling multiple websites, screenshots in a group chat, and endless back-and-forth, TeeSync handles everything in one place — from finding availability and polling your crew, to booking and sending reminders — all inside iMessage.

Yes — TeeSync is an iPhone app. The host (the person organizing the round) needs to have TeeSync installed. Once the host sends out a TeeSync invitation through iMessage, everyone in the group can accept or decline directly in the chat. The host then books the tee time for the whole group based on who's in.

The host picks a course, date, and tee time, then sends a TeeSync invitation to the group via iMessage. Each person accepts or declines — that's it. Once responses are in, the host confirms and TeeSync books the tee time for everyone. No coordinating across multiple apps, no one left out of the loop.

Yes. TeeSync handles all the follow-up automatically. Once a tee time is booked, everyone gets a confirmation. Leading up to the round, TeeSync sends reminders so nobody forgets — including a weather update the morning of. Payment reminders are also sent so the group stays on top of any outstanding costs.

TeeSync ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the TeeSync iPhone app and related services (the "Service"). By using TeeSync, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information — your name, phone number, and email address when you create an account or join the waitlist.
  • Booking information — the courses, dates, tee times, and group members associated with rounds you organize or join.
  • Payment information — processed securely through third-party payment providers. We do not store full credit or debit card numbers on our servers.
  • Contacts — if you choose to invite others, we access only the contacts you select in order to send invitations. We do not upload or store your full address book.
  • Usage and device data — app interactions, device type, iOS version, and diagnostic information used to operate and improve the Service.
  • Location data — approximate location, only if you grant permission, to show nearby courses and provide weather updates.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and operate the Service, including coordinating and booking tee times, sending invitations through iMessage, delivering confirmations, reminders, weather updates, and payment notifications, providing customer support, and improving the app. We do not sell your personal information.

3. How We Share Your Information

We share information only as needed to deliver the Service: with golf courses to complete bookings, with payment processors to handle transactions, with group members you invite (limited to what's needed to coordinate a round), and with service providers who support our infrastructure. We may also disclose information if required by law.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service and comply with our legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (see "Your Rights" below).

5. Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal information, and you may revoke permissions such as contacts and location through your iPhone settings. To request account deletion or exercise other rights, contact us at info@tee-sync.com.

6. Children's Privacy

TeeSync is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Security
