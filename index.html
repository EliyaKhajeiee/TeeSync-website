Why TeeSync

Book Smarter. Not Harder.

Planning a round with your group shouldn't take longer than the round itself. TeeSync handles the coordination so you can focus on the game.

Transparent, Course-Direct Pricing

TeeSync never marks up tee times. All prices come directly from the course — what you see is exactly what you pay. No surprise charges at checkout.

Built-In Course Search

Browse courses, check availability, and compare pricing without ever opening another tab. Everything is built directly into your iMessage thread.

Automatic Group Coordination

No more "are we still on?" threads. TeeSync collects everyone's availability, sends confirmations, and handles reminders — all automatically.

The Idea

Built because no one else fixed it.

Trying to book a tee time with four people is a logistical mess. You're coordinating schedules over text, checking course websites one by one, and somehow still showing up to the wrong time.

TeeSync started from a simple question: why can't this all just happen inside the group chat? No new apps. No separate logins. Just a clean, fast booking flow that lives where the conversation already is — iMessage.

Beyond group booking, TeeSync aggregates tee times from courses across Southern California into one platform — so you can actually compare what's available instead of checking five different websites.
