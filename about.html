Our Story

Built by Golfers,
for Golfers.

TeeSync started from a shared frustration — planning a round with friends shouldn't feel like coordinating a business trip.

The Problem We're Solving

Golf is easy.
Booking isn't.

Ask any regular golfer and they'll tell you the same thing: getting four people to agree on a course, a time, and a price takes more effort than the round itself. You're cross-referencing multiple websites, sending screenshots in the group chat, and by the time everyone confirms, the good spots are gone.

TeeSync was built to fix exactly that. We aggregate tee time availability from courses across Southern California into one place, so you can search, compare, and book without the runaround. And because the whole thing lives inside iMessage, your group doesn't need to download anything — they just respond in the chat.

Our goal is simple: make it easier for real golfers to get on the course. Less searching. Less back-and-forth. More rounds actually happening.
